Where Does Kid Yoga Take Place?

It doesn't hurts to start in your own home. If you practice Yoga in the comfort of your home, why not have your child follow along. You could show them a few of your favorite poses, or just have them watch. They’ll be impressed by your skill, and this could be a great parent child bonding moment. There are also quite a few Yoga for kids studios sprouting up around the country. Karma Kids Yoga in New York in particular is paving the way for Yoga kids to grow. They’ve coined the term “play yoga” which allows children to enjoy yoga on their own level, and gain body control and mind connection.

Meditation is the key to calm your mind, body and spirit.
